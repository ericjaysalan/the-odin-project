Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • salt
  • water
  • oil

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes, cut into thin slices and submerge in cold water.
  2. Add salt to taste to the water
  3. Fill a deep pan with oil for deep frying
  4. Deep fry potatoes until golden borwn
  5. Let cool, salt to taste
